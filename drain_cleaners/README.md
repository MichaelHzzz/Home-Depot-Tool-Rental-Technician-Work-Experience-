# Drain Cleaners

## Equipment Overview
Portable electromechanical equipment used for clearing obstructions in drainage
and plumbing systems. These tools combine electric motors, mechanical drive
cables, and safety-protected power connections for controlled operation.

## Maintenance Performed
- Inspected electrical components, power cords, switches, and safety features for proper operation
- Replaced GFCI-protected power plugs as required to restore electrical safety and reliability
- Inspected mechanical drive cables for tangling, wear, or damage and corrected cable routing issues
- Cut and replaced worn or damaged cable tips to restore proper cutting and clearing performance
- Cleaned equipment and inspected rotating components for smooth operation
- Performed routine preventive maintenance to ensure safe and reliable operation

## Verification
- Conducted functional testing under normal operating conditions
- Verified smooth cable rotation, proper control response, and safe electrical operation
- Confirmed equipment met safety and operational requirements before return to service
