# Dirt Compactors

## Equipment Overview
Electromechanical compaction equipment used in construction and landscaping
applications. These tools combine electric power, mechanical motion, and
operator controls to deliver controlled compaction performance.

## Maintenance Performed
- Inspected electrical components, power connections, and control interfaces for proper operation
- Checked mechanical assemblies and moving components for wear, damage, or obstruction
- Cleaned equipment and removed debris that could affect performance
- Performed routine preventive maintenance to ensure safe and reliable operation

## Verification
- Conducted functional testing under normal operating conditions
- Verified equipment responded correctly to controls and operated smoothly
- Confirmed equipment met safety and operational requirements before return to service
