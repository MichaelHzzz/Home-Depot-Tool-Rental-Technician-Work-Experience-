# Electromechanical Tool Diagnostics & Maintenance Portfolio

This repository documents hands-on experience inspecting, troubleshooting,
and maintaining electromechanical rental equipment. The work shown here
emphasizes preventive maintenance, system-level diagnostics, and functional
verification to ensure safe and reliable operation.

This portfolio supports my work experience as a Tool Rental Associate and
demonstrates practical engineering skills relevant to electrical and hardware
engineering roles.

---

## Equipment Documented

- **Chainsaws**  
  Ignition system servicing, air intake maintenance, spark plug replacement,
  and functional verification.

- **Dirt Compactors**  
  Electrical and mechanical inspection, preventive maintenance, and operational
  testing under normal conditions.

- **Drain Cleaners**  
  Inspection of electrical controls and mechanical drive systems, cleaning,
  and functional verification.

- **Pressure Washers**  
  Spark plug replacement, filter cleaning, pull-cord (recoil starter) repair,
  hose and fitting inspection, and pressure output verification.
