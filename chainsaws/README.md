# Chainsaws

## Equipment Overview
Portable gasoline-powered electromechanical cutting equipment used for
construction, landscaping, and maintenance applications.

## Maintenance Performed
- Inspected ignition, fuel delivery, and air intake systems for proper operation
- Cleaned and serviced air filter and cutting components
- Replaced spark plug as required to restore reliable ignition
- Performed routine preventive maintenance to ensure safe operation

## Verification
- Conducted functional testing after maintenance
- Verified engine startup, throttle response, and cutting operation
- Confirmed equipment met safety and operational requirements before return to service
