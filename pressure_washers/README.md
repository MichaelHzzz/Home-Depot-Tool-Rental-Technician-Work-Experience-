# Pressure Washers

## Equipment Overview
Portable gasoline-powered electromechanical cleaning equipment used to deliver
high-pressure water for construction, maintenance, and surface cleaning
applications.

## Maintenance Performed
- Inspected ignition, fuel delivery, and air intake systems for proper operation
- Replaced spark plugs to restore reliable ignition and engine startup
- Cleaned and serviced air and intake filters to ensure proper airflow
- Inspected and repaired pull-cord (recoil starter) mechanisms as required
- Checked hoses, fittings, and spray assemblies for wear, leaks, or damage
- Performed routine preventive maintenance to ensure safe and reliable operation

## Verification
- Conducted functional testing after maintenance
- Verified engine startup, throttle response, and pressure output
- Confirmed trigger operation and water flow under normal operating conditions
- Ensured equipment met safety and operational requirements before return to service
